Privacy Policy

Sehaj Breath — Last updated February 9, 2026

Overview

Sehaj Breath is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe your personal practice is yours alone.

Data Collection

Sehaj Breath does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information — including your session history, journal entries, and settings — is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools. There are no cookies, no fingerprinting, and no third-party SDKs that collect data.

In-App Purchases

The app offers optional donation-based in-app purchases processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

Sehaj Breath does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not require or request any personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is ever updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
